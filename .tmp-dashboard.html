Billing FAQ

How are credits calculated?▼
1 credit equals 1 minute of AI call time or 10 chat messages. SMS follow-ups cost 2 credits each. Credits never expire as long as your account is active.
Can I get a refund for unused credits?▼
Yes, unused credits from the current billing period can be refunded within 14 days of purchase. Please contact our support team for assistance.
What happens when I run out of credits?▼
Your AI agent will continue to operate but interactions will be limited. You'll receive email notifications at 20%, 10%, and 5% remaining credits.
Can I change my plan anytime?▼
Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle. Unused credits carry over.
Are there enterprise plans available?▼
Yes, we offer custom enterprise plans for businesses with high-volume needs. Contact our sales team at enterprise@versafic.com for a tailored quote.
