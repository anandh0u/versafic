## 🏢 COMPANY/ABOUT (For Razorpay Signup - 150 words)

**Company Name**: Versafic  
**Business Type**: SaaS Platform  
**Industry**: AI Customer Service & Communication Automation

Versafic is a B2B SaaS platform that enables restaurants, hotels, clinics, and service companies to automate customer communications using AI. Our platform handles customer phone calls, voice queries, and chat support 24/7, allowing businesses to provide better service while reducing operational costs.

We operate on a transparent credit-based billing model where businesses purchase credits (₹1 = 10 credits) and consumption is tracked per usage: AI chat messages (5 credits), call minutes (20 credits), and voice processing (2-5 credits). Businesses can top up credits instantly via Razorpay or enable auto-recharge.

Target customers are SMBs and enterprises in hospitality, healthcare, retail, and professional services who need scalable, affordable AI customer support.

**Revenue Model**: Credit-based SaaS with usage billing  
**Average Transaction**: ₹99 - ₹499 per top-up

## 💼 BUSINESS MODEL (100 words)

Versafic operates a B2B SaaS model with credit-based billing:

**Revenue Streams:**
1. Credit purchase packs (₹99/₹199/₹499)
2. Usage consumption (5-20 credits per service)
3. Optional auto-recharge subscriptions

**Pricing:** 
- Starter: ₹99 = 990 credits
- Growth: ₹199 = 1,990 credits
- Pro: ₹499 = 4,990 credits

**Usage Costs:**
- AI chat: 5 credits per message (₹0.50)
- Voice call: 20 credits per minute (₹2)
- Voice processing: 2-5 credits per action

This ensures transparent pricing while aligning revenue with actual value delivered. Businesses pay only for what they use.

## 📝 FAQ RESPONSES

**Q: How much does a call cost?**  
A: Voice calls cost 20 credits per minute = ₹2 per minute. A typical 2-minute call costs ₹4. Compare that to hiring staff at ₹15,000/month.

**Q: What if I run out of credits?**  
A: Enable auto-recharge and we'll automatically top up when your balance falls below the threshold. Service never stops.

**Q: Can the AI speak Hindi?**  
A: Yes! Our AI supports Hindi, English, and several regional languages. It understands Indian accents and context.

**Q: Is this a subscription or pay-per-use?**  
A: Pay-per-use. Buy credits when you need them (₹99/₹199/₹499) and use them as needed. No monthly commitment.

**Q: How do I know credits are being used correctly?**  
A: Every transaction is logged in your dashboard. See exactly when, where, and how many credits were used for each call or chat.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**  
A: Unused credits remain in your wallet forever. We offer refunds within 7 days if you're not satisfied.

## 🎨 BRAND VOICE GUIDELINES

**Tone**: Professional yet approachable, confident but not arrogant

**Do's:**
- ✅ Use simple language (avoid jargon)
- ✅ Focus on business outcomes (save time, increase revenue)
- ✅ Be transparent about pricing
- ✅ Show empathy for SMB pain points
- ✅ Use Indian rupees and context
- ✅ Provide concrete examples

**Don'ts:**
- ❌ Don't use tech buzzwords unnecessarily
- ❌ Don't overpromise or hype
- ❌ Don't compare directly to competitors
- ❌ Don't hide pricing or terms
- ❌ Don't use complex English
